About Us

We, Rustic Hostel located in Melbourne, Australia, state offer a pocket friendly stay to each patron and welcome them with complete warmth and hospitality. We offer an array of all essential services that are rendered by the hostel for a hassle free stay at no extra costs. For accommodation, the property offers spacious, airy and well-lit rooms, featuring sophisticated and welcoming ambience with the warmth and comfort of home. We take utmost care of the safety and security of individuals and their belongings staying in our hostel.
